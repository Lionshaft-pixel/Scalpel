Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 29, 2025

Privacy Policy for Scalpel

This Privacy Policy describes how Scalpel ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our bulk file renaming tool available at https://glitchyn.online.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 No Personal Data Collection

Scalpel is designed with privacy in mind. Our core file renaming functionality:

  • Does NOT collect any personal information
  • Does NOT use cookies
  • Does NOT track your activities
  • Processes all files locally in your browser

1.2 Feedback Form Data

When you voluntarily submit feedback through our form, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Rating (1-5)
  • Your feedback message

This data is processed through Formspree and is used solely to improve our service.

1.3 Analytics

We collect only basic analytics data:

  • Page visit counts (to understand usage patterns)

No personal identifiers or detailed tracking information is collected.

2. File Processing

Scalpel operates entirely client-side:

  • No file storage: Your files are never sent to our servers
  • Local processing: All file renaming happens in your browser using JavaScript
  • Immediate download: Renamed files are downloaded directly to your device
  • No server access: We cannot access, view, or store your files

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Formspree: For processing feedback form submissions
  • GitHub Pages: For hosting our website

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not:

  • Sell, trade, or rent your personal information
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Use your data for marketing purposes

The only data shared is feedback form submissions with Formspree, as necessary to process your feedback.

5. Data Security

Since we don't store your personal data or files, there's minimal security risk. However, we maintain:

  • Secure HTTPS connections
  • Regular security updates
  • Minimal data collection practices

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any personal data we might have (feedback submissions)
  • Request deletion of your feedback data
  • Opt-out of future communications

To exercise these rights, contact us through our feedback form.

7. Children's Privacy

Scalpel is suitable for all ages and contains no age-restricted content. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our feedback form.

10. Consent

By using our website, you consent to our Privacy Policy.
