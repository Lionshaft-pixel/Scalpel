How Scalpel Works

Learn how to easily rename your files with our simple, secure, and efficient process

1

Select Your Files

Choose the files you want to rename. Scalpel supports multiple files at once to save you time. Simply drag and drop or use our intuitive file selector.

2

Customize Naming Rules

Choose how you want your files renamed. You can add prefixes, suffixes, numbering, or completely new names. Our interface makes it easy to preview changes before processing.

3

Process & Download

Click the "Process Files" button. Your files will be renamed and prepared for download. After processing, you'll see a "Thank You" page with a download link.

4

Safety & Privacy

Scalpel does not store your files. Everything is processed in your browser or temporarily on the server for processing only, then deleted immediately. Your privacy is our priority.

5

Monetization

To keep this service free, ads are displayed on the download page. Watching the ad allows us to maintain the tool without charging users. We strive to keep ads unobtrusive.
